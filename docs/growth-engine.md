# SCRIMED Commercial Growth Engine

SCRIMED Commercial Growth Engine is the execution lane for turning proof depth into buyer motion. It organizes priority growth plays, conversion lanes, revenue proof steps, bottlenecks, owners, proof routes, and retained gates.

Public routes:

- `/growth-engine`
- `/api/growth-engine`
- `/api/growth-engine/brief`

## Boundary

The Commercial Growth Engine is growth execution readiness only. It is not a customer revenue guarantee, investment advice, securities offering material, audited financial reporting, valuation assurance, legal advice, tax advice, reimbursement assurance, procurement approval, customer permission, security certification, regulatory approval, PHI processing approval, production connector approval, or live clinical care authorization.

## Operating Use

1. Use `/growth-engine` to prioritize founder-led assessment outreach and synthetic pilot pipeline work.
2. Attach every buyer conversation to one proof route, one conversion event, and one disqualifier.
3. Keep buyer-specific artifacts behind protected release-control gates until customer permission and external review are retained.
4. Route fundraising, valuation, securities, tax, legal, reimbursement, PHI, security, connector, and live-care claims through qualified reviewers.
