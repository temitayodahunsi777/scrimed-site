## Interoperability Principle

Interoperability is a top-tier strategic priority. SCRIMED should build connectors, not silos, across EHRs, payers, hospitals, clinics, governments, public health systems, wearables, medical devices, research platforms, data warehouses, FHIR ecosystems, HL7 ecosystems, and future healthcare standards.

## Security Principle

Assume adversarial environments, misuse attempts, prompt injection attempts, insider threats, compliance risks, and data leakage risks. Apply Zero Trust, least privilege, role-based access control, audit logging, encryption, monitoring, governance controls, identity management, and secure-by-default design.

## Agent Strategy

SCRIMED is agent-native. Prefer specialized governed agents over giant general-purpose agents. Every agent must be governed, auditable, explainable, secure, observable, human-supervised, role-scoped, and interoperable.

## Operating Models

FaithCore is the spiritually aligned support and trust layer. FaithCore can support dignity, encouragement, ethical governance, and whole-person care, while never replacing clinical judgment, medical guidance, emergency care, consent, or professional standards.
